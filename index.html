Moraforesight 1.0 is a novel initiative by the
IEEE Student Branch of the University of Moratuwa.

It will culminate in a three-day camp, during
which students will be provided the opportunity to
participate in sessions conducted by industry-
leading professionals and lecturers.

Through this project, we intend to provide
exclusive educational resources and mentoring to a
select group of 100 students, in fields such as
Programming, Robotics and Entrepreneurship.

IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is a
professional association that focuses on advancing technology for
the benefit of humanity. It is the world's largest technical
professional organization and provides a platform for researchers,
engineers, and technologists to collaborate, share ideas, and
develop new technologies. IEEE publishes numerous technical
journals and organizes conferences, workshops, and other events
to promote the exchange of knowledge and ideas in the field of
electrical and electronics engineering.
